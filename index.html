In an exciting new project, the Team at BIOZONE is developing a rich collection of 3D models that will be a fantastic addition to your classroom teaching assets. We have developed annotated 3D models that allow students to explore biological concepts on their own devices. Go full screen, rotate the model freely, zoom in and out, and click on the carefully researched labels attached to important features.

Ways to use the 3D models:

    • TEACHER : demonstrate the models to your class using a smart board or digital projector

    • STUDENTS : use your own devices to explore the structural features of these great models
